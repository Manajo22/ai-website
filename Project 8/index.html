About Work

Luminiscentia

Medium: Injection-molded plastic, LED, gravity sensor
Dimensions: Handheld scale
Year: 2021

Sparked by a serendipitous roadside encounter during a road trip across Australia, Luminiscentia is a meditative response to the fleeting brilliance of the light bug. Merging playfulness with precision, this handheld lamp reimagines bioluminescence through industrial design. Engineered with a gravity-activated switch, the lamp illuminates only when lifted—echoing the ephemeral glow of its living counterpart. Its form, rendered in glossy black plastic with subtle anatomical accuracy, invites both wonder and introspection. Luminiscentia is less a utility and more a poetic gesture: a fleeting light held gently between function and memory.
