About Work

Scale Light

Medium: Powder-coated aluminum, LED array
Dimensions: Custom-fitted
Year: 2022

Crafted as part of a bespoke lighting commission for a seafood-focused restaurant, Scale Light is a wall-mounted fixture that subtly echoes marine life through its textural and formal language. The surface, patterned with a delicate fish scale motif, refracts light softly to create an underwater ambience—calm, immersive, and intentional. Designed to complement a primary lighting piece, Scale Light operates in visual tandem while standing firmly on its own, signaling the establishment's culinary expertise without overt symbolism. It’s a quiet celebration of craft, cuisine, and coastal reverence.
