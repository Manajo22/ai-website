Midnight in Miami

Medium:Woven jute
Type:Suspended light sculpture

Crafted from humble jute—a fiber synonymous with Miami’s sun-soaked markets and coastal ease—Midnight in Miami transforms the everyday into something celestial. In this large-scale suspended form, Moi Faux weaves the language of tote bags and beach rituals into a constellation of light and shadow. The undulating shape, airy and organic, floats like a breeze off Biscayne Bay, while its perforations scatter light like stars across the room. Despite its vast scale, the work exudes softness and familiarity, anchoring itself in the spirit of home. This piece is a tribute to the unassuming materials of daily life and the quiet rituals that give a place its soul. It evokes Miami not just as a geography, but as a feeling—warm, woven, luminous, and deeply personal. A sky made of fabric, Midnight in Miami invites the viewer to dream wide awake.
